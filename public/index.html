About ChatLog

ChatLog is a modern, secure, and user-friendly chat platform designed to connect people instantly. Whether you want to chat with friends, collaborate with colleagues, or communicate with clients—ChatLog offers a seamless messaging experience with built-in privacy and performance.

Our goal is to make real-time communication easy, fast, and enjoyable. We offer features like one-on-one chats, login/signup authentication, Google sign-in, responsive design, and more—all built using cutting-edge technologies like MERN Stack and Bootstrap 5.

Designed with simplicity and speed in mind, ChatLog ensures that your conversations are smooth and secure. We're constantly working on new features to make ChatLog better every day.
